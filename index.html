We're Dads and we play old school LAN games together every month in cities across Australia. It's a free event and a great way to meet up other Dads regularly.

Our community is on Discord and is full of interesting discussions about technology, dad life, venting, advice, and more.

No computer to bring along? No problem! We have partnered with WorkVentures who have given us dozens of spare laptops, so there's no barrier for you to join in.

  • Battlefield 2
  • Unreal Tournament 2004
  • Quake 3
