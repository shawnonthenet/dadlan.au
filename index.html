DadLAN Australia

Being a Dad is great, but it can also be socially isolating and challenging. We get together across the country every month for a few hours to play some games. It's a good way to connect with other Dads and maybe talk about other things too.

Join a LAN event in Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, Adelaide, or remotely.

Games we're playing

  • Battlefield 1942
  • Unreal Tournament 2004
  • Quake 3
  • Warcraft 3

We discuss new games on the Discord.

Join our Discord to chat and book a place at the next event (it's free).
