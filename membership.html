Membership

DadLAN LTD. offers a yearly membership for people wanting to be an official part of the organisation.

DadLAN LTD. Constitution

  • Participation in DadLAN LTD. events is free, and will always be free
  • Membership to DadLAN LTD. is A$149 + GST/year
    • Membership fees are not tax-deductible

Membership fees help DadLAN LTD. with (but not limited to):

  • Venue hires for subnets
  • Administration fees (e.g. Office 365)
  • Marketing (e.g. stickers)
  • Advertising (e.g. Facebook Ads)

Read about your obligations and rights of being a member before committing.

Thank you for considering being a member of DadLAN LTD. and supporting its growth.
